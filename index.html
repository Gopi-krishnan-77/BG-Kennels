About Us

Welcome to BG Kennel, your trusted home away from home for furry family members. Nestled in the heart of Bengaluru, BG Kennel has been a beloved part of the local pet community since 2003. Our journey began with a simple yet powerful idea: to create a safe, loving, and fun environment for dogs while their owners are away.

Our Mission and Values

At BG Kennel, our mission is clear: to provide the highest standard of care and comfort for every dog entrusted to us. We believe in fostering a happy, healthy, and secure atmosphere where tails wag, and paws play freely. Our core values center around compassion, integrity, and a relentless dedication to the well-being of every four-legged guest.

Our Services

Boarding

Our spacious and comfortable kennels provide a secure and cozy home-away-from-home for your furry friend. Whether it's a weekend getaway or an extended vacation, your dog will be well-cared-for by our experienced staff.

Daycare

Keep your dog active and engaged while you're at work or running errands. Our dog daycare service offers a fun and safe environment for socialization, playtime, and exercise.

Professional Grooming

Treat your dog to a spa day! Our professional grooming services include baths, haircuts, nail trims, and more, ensuring your pet looks and feels their best.

Training Classes

From basic obedience to advanced behavior modification, our certified trainers can help your dog become a well-behaved companion. We offer group classes and one-on-one sessions tailored to your dog's needs.

Outdoor Play and Exercise

Dogs need exercise to stay happy and healthy. Our outdoor play areas and supervised walks provide dogs with ample opportunities to run, play, and burn off energy.

Specialized Care for Seniors

Older dogs deserve extra care and attention. We specialize in senior dog care, offering comfortable accommodations and tailored activities to meet the unique needs of senior pets.

Medication Administration

If your dog requires medication, our trained staff can administer it per your veterinarian's instructions, ensuring your pet's health and well-being.

Veterinary Care Access

Your dog's health is our priority. We have easy access to a nearby veterinary clinic for any emergencies or medical needs, providing you with peace of mind during your pet's stay.
